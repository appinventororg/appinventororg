Name and define three ways in which the Android platform is more open than iOS (iPhone). Which of these is key to why App Inventor was developed for Android?

Answer

Programmers can access the source (program) code and change it making a new app. (it is Android itself which is open source)

   Any companies can make mobile devices for Android.    Android is an open distribution meaning it is easy to publish on the market (no curation) and can upload anywhere such as the web. The third way is key as to why App Inventor was developed for Android.
