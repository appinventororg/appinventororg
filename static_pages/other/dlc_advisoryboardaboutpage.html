Democratizing Computing Lab Advisory About Page

It is the mission of the Democratizing Computing Lab to break the digital divide between the tech-savy "elite",who understand programming code, and everyone else. In particular, the Democratizing Computing Lab Advisory Board aims to get more young people involved in learning the technology that they are surrounded with. Through the use of successes such as App inventor and The Hidden Genuis Project, the advisory board strives to educate the minds of today, for a better, more technologically-advanced tomorrow.

David Wolber

Director, Democratize Computing Lab at University of San Francisco
