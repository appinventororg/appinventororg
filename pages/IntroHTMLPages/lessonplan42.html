Lesson Plan 4.2: List Iteration:
Conceptualize and Customize

Printable Version

Objective

Reinforce understanding of list iteration

Preparation

print out the conceptualize and customize worksheet:

https://docs.google.com/document/d/1uZAumHBz_Df2ZJhgelbxqM2VXONmQxNeomIgaYOI5A8/

1. Conceptualize and Paper-Code (30)

Handout the worksheet and break the students into small discussion groups. The first question involves reading code and being able to trace what happens. It also provides blocks they’ll need for the code sketching of the other problems.

2. Customize (30-90)

The customization problems involve changing the slideshow in ways that require the student to iterate backwards (PreviousButton) and perform different bounds checks (e.g., when they reach the first question). Customizing the app in this way is essential for truly understanding the very important fundamental of list iteration with an index.

There are also some fun customizations including one in which the students create a slideshow game with the pictures of all their classmates. This is fun and will give students a chance to work with media they create.

The slideshow, including the suggested “Meet your Classmates” app, uses static data-- the programmer takes the pictures and uploads them into the app. This can lead to an interesting discussion about apps with dynamic data, e.g., one where the app user takes pictures with the app which are put into the slideshow. You may have some advanced students who can try this, or just use it as a way to motivate the upcoming lessons in the next module.
