Set Up Your Google Voice Account

To create a Google Voice account,

  1. On your computer, go to voice.google.com
  2. Follow the instructions there to create a Google Voice number and activate your account.

Installing the Google Voice App

To download and install Google voice on your phone:

  1. On your device, Start the phone's Market or Play Store app.
  2. Search for "Google Voice".
  3. Download and install the app.
  4. Read and approve the app's permission requests.
  5. Start the app and follow the setup instructions.
  6. Set the following Google Voice settings under Voice > Sync and notifications:
  • Receive text messages > Via the Google Voice app
  • Text notifications > Off (unless you want your app and Google Voice to notify you)

Test it

Have someone send a text to your google voice number. Do you see it at Google voice on your computer? Do you receive it on your phone?
